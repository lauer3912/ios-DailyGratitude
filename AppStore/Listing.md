**Start your gratitude journey today.**

DailyGratitude helps you build a lasting gratitude habit through daily journaling, structured challenges, and mood tracking. Join thousands who have transformed their perspective and found more joy in everyday life.

**Features:**

✨ **Daily Gratitude Prompts**
Fresh, thoughtful prompts every day to spark reflection and help you discover new things to appreciate.

📅 **21-Day Gratitude Challenge**
Build a lasting habit with our scientifically-backed challenge. Track your progress day by day and celebrate milestones.

📊 **Mood Tracking**
Monitor your emotional wellbeing with simple daily mood checks. See trends over time and understand what affects your happiness.

📖 **Beautiful Journal**
Browse your past entries in calendar or list view. Search, filter, and revisit the moments that mattered.

🏆 **Achievement System**
Earn badges for your progress — from first entry to 30-day streaks. Celebrate your wins!

📱 **Home Screen Widget**
Keep your streak visible with our widget. Quick glance, daily reminder.

🌙 **Dark Mode**
Designed with a premium dark theme for comfortable nighttime journaling.
