Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

DailyGratitude is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your gratitude journal should remain private and personal.

Data Collection

DailyGratitude does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your gratitude entries, mood data, and progress information are stored exclusively on your device.

Local Storage

Your journal entries and preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and on-device storage. This data never leaves your device.

No Account Required

DailyGratitude does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking systems, or third-party services that collect user data.

Export Feature

You can export your journal entries at any time using the in-app export feature. This allows you to back up or transfer your data.

Children's Privacy

DailyGratitude is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store.
